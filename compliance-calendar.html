New Obligations to Watch
AFSL – ASIC
ChangeBreach Reporting – Hard 30-Day Deadline
No extensions under CA 912DAA / RG 78. Clock starts from knowledge of or recklessness as to the breach. Calibrate the Breaches Register escalation workflow.
DeadlineFS70 / FS71 Audit Lodgement – 31 Oct 2026
Annual financial statements and audit report must be lodged within 4 months of 30 June FY close. Engage auditor now. (CA 989A–D, AFSL Condition)
ChangeConflicts of Interest – Enhanced ASIC Scrutiny 2026
Ensure Conflicts Register captures perceived conflicts and FSG/SOA disclosure language is current. (CA 912A(1)(aa))
AUSTRAC – AML/CTF Reforms 2026
CheckCompliance Officer Notification – 30 May 2026
Confirm AUSTRAC notification of your AML/CTF Compliance Officer is complete and records are current.
Deadline – 29 Jul 2026Tranche 2 Enrolment
Lawyers, accountants, real estate professionals newly regulated from 1 Jul 2026. Update counterparty CDD procedures. (s51B AML/CTF Act)
New – 1 Jul 2026Travel Rule – Virtual Asset Transfers
VASPs must exchange customer information on virtual asset transfers. Assess whether fund activities trigger this obligation.
Live – 31 Mar 2026Enhanced Ongoing CDD Standard
Updated CDD obligations already in effect. Confirm transaction monitoring and training are updated to the new standard.
AER – Australian Energy Regulator (Upcoming)
Watch – 1 Jul 2026AER Separates from ACCC
AER becomes fully independent. Monitor transition communications for changes to reporting portals and contact points.
New – 1 Jul 2026Enhanced Concession Disclosure (Retail Code)
Retailers must provide more frequent concession/rebate information. Update practices if portfolio companies hold energy retail authorisations.
Deadline – 31 Aug 2026Half-Yearly Breach Reports
AER-regulated entities submit half-yearly compliance/breach reports by 28 Feb and 31 Aug each year.
Watch – Dec 2026Hardship Customer Pricing Controls
Hardship customers must pay no more than the "deemed better offer". Controls tested in assurance audits after this date.
